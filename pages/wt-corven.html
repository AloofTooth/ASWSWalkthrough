Hunter in the woods.

  1. Buy him a drink.
  2. After you've given him a drink you can also talk to him in the ruins north of the hunting ground.
  3. Hunt a rabbit to get a hare paw.
  4. Bring the paw to him, this will increase your hunting skill by one point.
  5. There is a threesome scene with him & Arianna, for details check Arianna's section of the walk-through.
