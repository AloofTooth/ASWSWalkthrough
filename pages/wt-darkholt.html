1. To get to this section follow Arianna's story about the cult.
  2. After rescuing Arianna return to the hideout underneath the church near the ruins at night and sneak into your bed in Saiel's room.
  3. The next morning talk to Saiel and together with him leave the hideout to arrive in Darkholt.
  4. After a short cutscene you are now tasked to deconstruct the ruins. To do so you must beat a small minigame. But if you don't like it you can completely skip it, here is how: clear the rubble in the center of darkholt (bones and carts). After clearing two of such you can choose to work lazy during the deconstruction of the ruins.
  5. Deconstruct enough buildings so that you have material to build the barracks in the NW of Darkholt.
  6. After the building is done talk to Scorial during dinner in the hideout. Saiel will be frustrated after that and reveal his identity to the MC.
  7. The MC now has the idea to cause a conflict with the orcs, for that get 3 ritual daggers from the hideout. You can find them at the 3 weapon stands in the south of the Hideout (also check the weaponstands near the cells).
  8. Now go and defeat 3 orcs and place the ritual daggers near the corpses.
  9. Return to Saiel another day and talk to him about going to Darkholt again. Be prepared for a fight with some orcs to save Vilina.
  10. Now in control over the rebuilding, focus on building the leaders quarters in the north, you will again require to deconstruct some ruins for it.
  11. After the building is done inspect it together with Saiel the next day, this will trigger a visit from Vilina.
  12. Now you can manage the rebuilding via the desk inside the quarters. From now on you can also use the map to fast-travel to Darkholt. Focus on gathering Velthaze in the forest and use the storage icons on the right side to increase or decrease the stock inside the village. Fields will require Velthaze to be planted, but if you reach 20% the Fields will continue to grow, even without workers. For more details check the ? In the top left.
  13. After constructing the warehouse enter the Leader's quarters during the day (while Saiel is standing next to it). This will trigger Vilina's next visit. Should you miss her visit use the Management map and click on the letter icon to send her another invitation.
  14. After her visit focus on producing Velthaze. You need to reach at least 20 reputation with the Cult before you can progress with Vilina.
  15. Invite her over again and improve your relationship with her until she asks you to modify your mask.
  16. Visit Roderic in his workshop and talk to him about the mask.
  17. Back in Darkholt invite Vilina via your desk. In the following scene you now have to decide which route you want to go with Vilina. The corruption route is only available if you progressed through Penny's story up to the point where you discovered Elisabeth's secret.
    • For the love route just follow along with Vilina's wishes.
    • For the corruption route tell her that you're tired. After she leaves talk to Saiel outside to get a letter from him.
  18. On Vilina's next visit you can now show her the letter and demand her help. You now also have the choice to not have any sexual interaction with her at all.
  19. Invite Vilina again to get more information about her background.
  20. The next time you invite Vilina she won't arrive as expected because of an ambush. If you fail to save her in time (past 6pm), she will have sex with one of the orcs (ntr on only).
  21. After the followup event you have now the following options depening on route:
    • Loveroute – during the next conversation with her she will ask you questions, if you raise too much suspicion (tell her about your father, and that you want revenge and you go into detail about the experiments) she will just take off your mask herself. Otherwise you will have the choice to reveal yourself to her, which will have consequences in the future on her behavior (if you reveal it before doing Penny's quest it will also change a lot of her dialog at the Coldstone Estate).
    • Corruption route – You can raise her corruption up to 10+ which will trigger a dialog about her accepting you as her master. Which then again changes her behavior.
  22. You can now visit Roderic again to report back about what you've found out. This is currently the end of the questline.
