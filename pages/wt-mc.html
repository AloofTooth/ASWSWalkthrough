1. To begin the quest first follow Mira's quest up to step 17 this will then start a new quest.
  2. To get to Kirlic you first need a horse and a place where it can stay, if you completed Kate's quest already then this step can be skipped (continue at step 5). Otherwise, talk to Penny and ask her if you can use her barn.
  3. Now to get the horse you'll need to convince Ophilia to give you hers. For that first win Blackjack against Grant (Merchant, who is at the city square on Monday and Tuesday) you can play with him inside the inn in the evening. Play until he has lost all his money, then he will offer you his donkey.
  4. With the donkey in the barn now go to Ophilia and trade it in for the horse. (You can trick her by poisoning the horse first but that is optional).
  5. Now when interacting with the horse the MC will notice that he's missing a saddle. For that interact with Penny and ask her about it. She has an old saddle from her father, but she won't give it to you unless you helped her on the fields a little or are in a relationship with her.
  6. The saddle is in a bad condition and needs to be repaired. You can do that at the workbench inside the MC's house. If you didn't build the workbench yet, you need to talk to Frisha, who will also teach you how to sew.
  7. Now you'll need at least 20 levels in Sewing before you can repair the saddle (you can see your current level in the MC's stats in the menu if you scroll down) – you can increase your skill by making armor at the workbench (working in the smithy won't level the sewing skill).
  8. Now after you've repaired the saddle return to the barn in the morning and saddle your horse. You can now go on a ride to Kirlic. It is advised to be level 30+ before you do that.
  9. You will make a stop at the old boar inn, where you will be informed that you can't enter Kirlic without the needed documents. So you decide to ask Bjorn for help with getting you inside the city. He will be against it and you first have to convince him. For how to do that check Kate's quest step 42.
  10. Before you can now get to Kirlic talk to Tessa at the Old Boar Inn, she will come with you.
  11. Now in the afternoon ask Bjorn to smuggle you into Kirlic, the next morning meet him outside the house near his wagon and begin the journey.
  12. After you arrive in Kirlic visit the inn at the marketplace, the golden goose and then talk to the Innkeeper Gevin. Before he will help you, you need to improve your relationship with him, to do that just ask for a room and rent it for at least a week.
  13. Now you can ask him about Renold, but before he will help you he wants another favor from you, you need to help his barmaid with a certain guest.
  14. Take a seat at the table before 8pm and then wait until the man enters. If you wait another hour you will see an interaction with Rose (the barmaid) and the nobleman. You now have to step in and help her. If you fail or ignore it the man will get touchy with Rose which will (if NTR is on) lead to her having sex with him after a few more of these fails. For the quest to continue you need to beat him up at least once. Since the scene will happen again this doesn't lock you from the content even if you beat him up the very first time.
  15. Now talk to Gevin again about Renold and he will point you to his contact, Kim, a boy in one of the SW alleys of the city.
  16. Talk to Kim near his tent and he will ask you to do something to improve the situation in the city. At the right side of the screen you can see your reputation inside the city, you will have to raise that reputation by helping beggars, the old man renovating buildings, or killing rogues inside the city. You can also help out women in need who are being robbed, there are 2 mini scenes (only sprite animations) for them if your reputation is high enough. You need at least 10 reputation in Kirlic before you can continue.
  17. Kim will inform you about a grave near the church that belongs to Renold, you now have to check that grave.
  18. Return to your room in the Golden Goose and interact with the bed.
  19. At night visit Rose's room. If you improved your relationship with her before by giving her tips when she served you to at least 5, you can start being intimate with her (if that isn't the case, no worry you can raise it later on the conversations will just be a little different).
  20. Now you have to enter the trapdoor inside the Kitchen of the inn and inside the sewers below go to the SW corner of the map.
  21. Return to the Golden Goose and tell Rose & Gevin that you're fine.
  22. You can now also build a stable at your camp (should you have progressed in Lyvia's story). To do that talk to the Horse-trader near the wall during the day, this will lead to the MC wanting to build a stable and when the stable is built you can talk to the trader again to buy horses for your companions. They will then join you on horseback after you returned to Arenfield (if you have them in group).

This is currently the end of the story and more will be added in the future.
