1. After you have renovated Emily's house visit yours. The MC will now think about renovating his own house.
  2. You will need some materials for that (see quest description) after you have gathered those and have rested you can start the work on your house in the morning.
  3. After the ground is renovated you have to also build the 1st floor in the same way.
  4. Talk to Roderic, he will offer you furniture for the house.
  5. Most important is the bathtub. To build it you must craft an oven at the smithy and you then need 5 iron bars (these are available if you already ventured into the mine, see John).
  6. For the kitchen you need a cauldron which also can be crafted at the forge in the smithy.
  7. Talk to Lucius about candles, from now on you are able to gather animal fat from killed animals, you can sell it for a good price to Lucius, after selling him 10 he will offer you candles in his shop (you have to wait 1 day).
  8. If you bought 20 of them enter your house to light everything up.
  9. If you interacted with the cat outside your door and build up affection on her this will open up the option to build a cat-door for it. Talk to Roderic about it.
  10. After Roderic built a new bed for you, you can invite some of the women to your house, including your companions if you have started a relationship with them. After you have first invited them seperately, you can then also invite them together, but only if they know about each other's relationship with the MC. you can achieve that by interacting with them during the party at the MC's camp (see sections in their progression). If they are all aware of each other new options to invite them together will show up. This will also later on allow you to invite them to your room at the inns should you have them with you.
